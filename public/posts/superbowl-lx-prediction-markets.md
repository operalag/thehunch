**WHY PREDICTION MARKETS ARE BETTER THAN SPORTSBOOKS**

Sportsbooks give you fixed odds with a built-in house edge (the vig). Prediction markets give you real-time price discovery driven by actual capital flow.

Consider:
- On Kalshi, Seahawks are priced at 68.5 cents. On Polymarket, 68.3 cents.
- Patriots are 32.5 cents on Kalshi, 31.8 cents on Polymarket.
- The spread between platforms: less than 0.7 cents.

That's tighter than most forex pairs. When professional money flows into transparent, open markets, you get better prices. Period. There's no bookmaker adjusting the line to protect their margin. There's just supply and demand.

Sportsbooks also close markets when they get nervous. Prediction markets stay open. If breaking news drops 30 minutes before kickoff -- a key player injury, a weather change, a coaching decision -- the market reprices in seconds. You can trade in and out. You can hedge. You can express a view on anything, not just what the sportsbook decides to offer.

This is what financial markets have always done. Prediction markets just apply the same principles to everything else.
